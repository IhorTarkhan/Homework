Train information at the station. The following data is stored for
each train: train number (four-digit integer); optional
the name of the train; type - international, high-speed, ordinary, suburban;
date and time of arrival at the station and departure from the station; level
the popularity of tickets for this train (the true number is the ratio
number of ticket requests to seats, may be less than 1 -
that is, some of the seats are empty, or more than 1 - that is, not everyone can
buy a ticket).

Search criteria:
trains with a name ending in a given snippet;
trains of a given type with a number in a given range;
trains with the date and time of departure not previously specified.
